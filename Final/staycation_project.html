Sundance:
The Perfect Vacation

Why Choose Sundance?

Sundance is mainly known for its skiing and snowboarding trails, and although these tails are pretty awesome, Sundance also offers plenty of activities in the summer as well. In the winter, it is a great stop for skiers and snowboarders of all skill levels, and provides plenty of trails for skiing. The location is also a bonus as it is very close to Provo, the scenic drive through the mountains only lasting about 15 minutes. In the Summer, the chances for adventure continue, and it holds many activities including ziplining, hiking and mountainbiking. Along with all the activities at Sundance, there are also a number of highly rated resturants that continue to please plenty of guests.

Some of the activities that Sundance offers:

Winter activities:

  • Skiing and snowboarding.
  • Ski school for begining skiers.
  • Multiple spas for relaxation.
  • Lots of places to eat.

Summer activities:

  • Countles trails for mountain biking, lifts included.
  • Trails for hiking and horseback riding.
  • Rides on the lifts that offer incrediable views.
  • Tours of the mountain on ziplines.
