Why I Like Math

My Reason

I enjoy math a lot. It's like an enjoyable puzzle for me that I can solve. While I'm not necissarily a math "wizard", I still enjow the idea of being able to solve things using math.

My Favorite Subject in Math

My favorite subject in math is algebra, because it is what works with equations the most. There are sometimes in math class, where I honestly start to miss algebra, and I often get excited when I see an algebraic problem on the homework every know and then.
